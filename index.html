What makes it different

Time as rings

Each year is a tree. Each entry adds growth. The older the tree, the more you can see the shape of that time in your life.

365 moments

One entry per day maximum. It forces you to find the single most important thing worth remembering.

Never forget a day

Sleeping trees remind you which years you were present. Archived years hold their shape forever.

Your forest, breathing.

Trees are alive. They breathe when you're active, rest when you pause, and archive when a year closes.

What your trees tell you
Living

Active within the last 14 days. The tree breathes. You are present.

Dormant

14–30 days without a new entry. The tree is resting. You can come back.

Archived

Over 30 days. The year is closed. The tree stays, preserving that time.
